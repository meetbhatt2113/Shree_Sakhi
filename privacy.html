Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Shree Sakhi exists to help girls and women ask sensitive questions without fear. That only works if we're genuinely careful with your information. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect, what we don't, and why.

What we never collect

  • Your name (unless you choose to type one into an optional field)
  • Your exact location (the "Find a Doctor" tool opens Google Maps in a new tab — we never see or store your address)
  • Your voice recordings (voice input is converted to text directly in your browser; the audio itself is never sent anywhere or stored)
  • Any tracking cookies for advertising

What we do collect, and why

Tracker data (cycle dates, pregnancy week, symptoms you tick): this is saved only in your own browser's local storage, on your own device. It is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Clearing your browser data or using a different device/browser will reset it.

Text of your questions to Sakhi AI: when you ask a question (by voice or typing), the text is sent to our AI service to generate a reply. We do not permanently log or store these conversations on our own servers.

The anonymous "Ask Sakhi" form: your question is sent to us so a real person can review and answer it in a future FAQ update. If you leave the optional contact field blank, we have no way to identify you. If you provide a phone/email, it is used only to send you a personal reply — never for marketing, and never shared with anyone else.

The "Request a callback" form: your phone number and preferred time are used only so a trained volunteer can call you back. This information is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Third parties involved

To make certain features work, some information passes through these services:

  • OpenRouter (AI provider): receives the text of your question to Sakhi AI in order to generate a response. It does not receive your name, location, or any other identifying information from us.
  • Google Maps: if you use "Find a Doctor," your search opens directly in Google Maps in a new tab — this is subject to Google's own privacy policy, not ours.

Your choices

  • You can use almost the entire site — FAQs, care guides, myths, tools — without submitting any personal information at all.
  • You can clear your tracker data at any time using the "Clear history" / "Reset" buttons within each tool.
  • You can skip the optional contact field on any form to stay fully anonymous.

Children's privacy

We know younger girls use this site, including for first-period information. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors beyond what's described above (which requires no name, account, or login at all). If you are a parent with a concern, please reach out using the contact details on our About page.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear, please use the "Ask Sakhi" form on the main site and mention it's about privacy — we'll get back to you.
