First Trimester (Weeks 1–13)

Focus on: starting folic acid, iron, and calcium as prescribed; gentle movement like walking; avoiding raw/undercooked food, alcohol, and smoking; managing nausea with small frequent meals.

Second Trimester (Weeks 14–27)

Focus on: prenatal yoga or continued walking; eating iron and calcium-rich foods (leafy greens, dairy, dal); attending regular checkups; starting to notice baby's movements around week 18-20.

Third Trimester (Weeks 28–40)

Focus on: tracking baby's kicks daily; preparing a hospital bag; practising slow breathing; watching for warning signs (severe headache, reduced movement, swelling) that need a doctor's attention right away; attending birth-prep or antenatal classes if available.

This guide is general and educational. Your own doctor's advice, based on your specific health and ultrasound dating, should always take priority over anything here. For the full interactive version with a live week calculator and AI-personalized insights, visit the Shree Sakhi website.
